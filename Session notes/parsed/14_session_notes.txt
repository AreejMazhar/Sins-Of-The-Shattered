Session Summary
The party finds themselves in a white void simulating the tower interior. They solve three puzzles inspired by Nailoth, Veldren, and family lessons: untangling a magical thread knot by finding and gently plucking the brightest anchor thread to reveal a sapphire key; retrieving an iron coin from a glass sphere using mage hand through a tiny hole; and beating a rigged shell game with spectral hands by discovering the rigged compartment, collecting pearls, and successfully stealing the bet coin with sleight of hand. The final puzzle involves refracting a white light beam into colored rays (blue, purple, red) matched to schools of magic (abjuration, illusion, evocation) per priorities: shield family, deceive enemy, strike when forced. A black iron door opens, yielding a letter from Veldren and a mysterious bypass device. Feeding magic into the device produces an illusory ID paper for one character, but warps and sucks in another, ending the session on a comedic cliffhanger.
