Session Summary
The party recapped defeating Titania and received goals to find Emily and decipher an Infernal contract in Waterdeep. They located Emily in the tavern after her ink overdose suicide attempt triggered by Astri's death, stabilized her with medicine and a healing potion, then had Boris fully heal her. Exploration revealed Valora's staff at the blacksmith, led to confronting Minerva holding a knife to a concubine linked to Leora's father Makarov's death and a cult-connected portrait. Lily was reunited with uncle Edmund at the laundromat via Ambrose. The session ended at the funeral with some party members on dates, discussing backstories over meals.
