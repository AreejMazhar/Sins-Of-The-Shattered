Session Summary
The party crossed the river and chose the left path via a wheel spin, entering a mysterious graveyard with a locked hut requiring three keys marked B, E, T. They solved puzzles by growing flowers on graves (Dave's, Prince Cedric's, Erica's), digging them up to retrieve keys, a crown, and broken sword. Discovered a slot machine in the hut that accepts permanent hit points or backstory memories for spins, with mostly duds but one jackpot pending. Found gold in water, scrolls in crypt, and a journal page revealing Astri's unspoken feelings for Emily before her death. Encountered Belial, an incubus kissing a corpse, who flirted aggressively using illusions before they retreated. Found a jar of 16 life essence orbs; used 6 on slot machine, kept 10. Technical issues with joining and lag persisted.
