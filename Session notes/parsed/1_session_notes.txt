Session Summary
The session began with technical setup issues in Foundry VTT, transitioning into character introductions in Worm's Rock prison. Each PC experienced vivid backstory flashbacks: Liora's patricidal storm surge, Alenia's wrongful arrest as an assassin, Vale's framing for sorcery, and Kiernan's vengeful killing of a noble abusing tiefling orphans. Neighbor Albert bantered suspiciously. Chaos erupted as Flaming Fist Gauntlet Valtor released them amid a fey invasion; butterflies slew guard Astri. Round 1 combat against three fey constructs (lantern mothkin, winged blaster, orb slapper) saw low rolls, pet contributions, and Valtor's aid. Post-fight exploration yielded potions, gold, letters (including Astri's admirer poems), scrolls, a staff, and hints like frosted lions riddle. Secret cavern revealed via crowbar. Albert's cell remained charmed shut despite insight revealing his forgotten status. Upstairs, allied stag druid (Boris), Valtor, knight, and Sir Kassadin battled more fey; party aided in chaotic darkness-infused fight. Discovered distressed child Lily grappling a fey via spell, mourning her slain mother. Looted regal greataxe, magic orb. Valtor escorted party toward lower city; Kassadin, Glinda, Boris pursued fey to crossing. Session ended with feedback on pacing, music, roleplay.
