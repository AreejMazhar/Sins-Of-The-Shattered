Session Summary
The party crash-lands in a swamp near Waterdeep via portal, with Durnehviir crushed under a Vale (ignored). They recognize the area from past smuggling routes into the city. Alenia emerges from pocket dimension. Hearing combat, they investigate and surprise-attack ghoulish Banwa Buso creatures assaulting Rovina, a Thieves' Guild vampire scout. After intense battle involving shrieks, bites, fireballs, and summons, they defeat the foes. Rovina explains new watch tightening control, plans to flee city. Party follows her through monster-infested sewage tunnel using stealth, illusions, and distractions to evade blind trolls and giant worm. They emerge in eerie, quiet Dark Ward. Learn Crawling Spider tavern seized; discuss seeking Dominique in Sea Ward or South Ward, consider guild passes but balk at 300gp cost each.
