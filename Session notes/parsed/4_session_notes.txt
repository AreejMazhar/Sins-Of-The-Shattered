Session Summary
The session was marred by severe technical lag and player disconnections during an intense boss fight against Titania, a fey creature with healing flowers and charm auras. Players repeatedly died, revived via spells like Healing Word and Mass Healing Word, and struggled with illusions, clones, and positioning. After destroying the flowers and discerning real Titania from illusions via cat checks and group guesses, they felled her with Synaptic Static, Eldritch Blast, and Chromatic Orbs. Her death revealed a betrayal vision (rotten maggot-filled peach), broke the Balduran statue's seal, and unleashed the Shard of Loyalty, which bound to the party's life force, draining it. A hooded figure failed to steal it as Duke Alder Raven Guard's troops arrived. Post-fight, the Duke delivered an inspiring speech on unity and rebuilding. Players engaged in light RP with NPCs like Ragnar the blacksmith (offered free weapon upgrade), Elizabeth the fashion designer, bartenders Lois and Dominique, and others, while worrying about missing Emily and the shard's implications.
