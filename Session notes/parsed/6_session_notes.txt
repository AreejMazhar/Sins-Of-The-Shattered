Session Summary
The party said emotional farewells to Boris and Glinda in Baldur's Gate, with Minerva joining their journey to Waterdeep. Boris provided a map highlighting two routes: a fast, dangerous path through the Field of the Dead and Trollclaws versus a slow, safer river path. After debate, they chose the perilous fast route. A mysterious shard pull tugged northwest but was ignored. In the Field of the Dead, they investigated foggy bones, cleared mist with mage hands, opened a trapped chest triggering ghoul combat, looted a wand, Disintegrate scroll, and bone whip. They crossed an ectoplasmic river haunted by skeletal spirits using jumps, whips, and spells, then found a Speak with Dead ring and 300 gold.
