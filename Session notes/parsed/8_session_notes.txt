Session Summary
The party begins with chaotic gambling on a spin wheel using life essence and HP, with Kanjak sacrificing 5 HP for mediocre prizes including a magical paint bucket and extendable rod. They explore the crypt, discovering marbles trapping prehistoric animals, releasing a loyal blind Siamese cat named Dunder Dunder. They find more life essence, an unconscious swordsman amid ghoul corpses, stabilize him, and store him in a pocket dimension. A mysterious moving painting of Josephine reveals lies about her nature, linked to Sir St. George. They stealthily loot gold-plated warlock armor and a sword from a sleeping creature's lair, noting a connection to George. Emerging into Trolls' Claw, they encounter a dying village plagued by drought and ghouls. They revive dehydrated villagers, uncover notes about Sophia and Jeffrey, find fresh food hoarded in one house, and fall through a trapdoor in another, rescuing mute Madeline who signs that village chief Jeffrey is hoarding supplies. They breach his secret underground base, confronting him with suppliers as the session ends.
